What is spatial biological data?

Spatial biological data seeks to not only look at what kinds of cells are present in a tissue sample, but where they are located. This is important because cell-cell interaction is the main driver for tissue functionality, especially in diseases such as cancer, where the tumor microenvironment is very complex.

In my work, I've looked at what cell location patterns are important in a variety of diseases, such as different types of cancer and fibrosis, see publications here.
